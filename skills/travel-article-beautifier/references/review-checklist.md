# Review Checklist

Run this before final delivery.

## Narrative

- The opening makes the reader want to continue.
- The article has a clear arc: route, discovery, contrast, or reflection.
- Scene details are specific and not generic.
- Transitions explain movement through place and time.

## Travel Accuracy

- No invented visits, restaurants, prices, hours, transport schedules, or current rules.
- User-provided dates, names, and place spellings are preserved.
- Unverified current facts are flagged.
- Advice is framed with appropriate caveats.

## Visual Polish

- The first screen communicates destination, mood, and article promise.
- Headings are scannable.
- Images or placeholders have meaningful captions.
- Practical blocks do not interrupt the narrative flow.
- Mobile layout is readable.

## Delivery

- Markdown is clean and portable.
- HTML is self-contained unless the user requested assets.
- File paths are provided for generated artifacts.
- Assumptions and missing source material are listed briefly.

## Quality Gates

When doing a full harness-managed run, apply `harness/quality-gates/travel-article-beautifier.yaml`. Any fail-severity gate must be fixed before final delivery.
