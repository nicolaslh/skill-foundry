# Travel Article Types

Use this reference to choose structure and section rhythm.

## Route Travelogue

Best for road trips, city walks, multi-city routes, train journeys, and day-by-day diaries.

Suggested sections:

- Opening scene
- Route overview
- Day or leg sections
- Best moments
- Practical notes
- Reflection or return

## Destination Essay

Best for one city, neighborhood, hotel, island, museum, or scenic area.

Suggested sections:

- Core impression
- Arrival
- What changed while exploring
- Signature details
- Practical visit notes
- Closing image

## Practical Guide With Narrative

Best when readers need both story and utility.

Suggested sections:

- Why go
- Suggested route
- Food, stay, transport, timing
- Mistakes to avoid
- Personal highlights
- Planning checklist

## Photo-Led Article

Best when the user provides images or asks for a visual article.

Suggested sections:

- Hero photo and caption
- Photo chapters
- Short narrative bridges
- Route/timeline
- Practical appendix

## Reflection Essay

Best for emotional, literary, or personal travel writing.

Suggested sections:

- Opening memory
- Place as mirror
- Key scenes
- Tension or discovery
- Quiet conclusion
