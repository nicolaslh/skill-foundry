# Style Template: City Magazine

- Version: 0.1.0
- Last updated: 2026-07-14

Use this prompt template when the article is about a city, neighborhood, weekend trip, cafe route, design hotel, shopping walk, restaurant crawl, or stylish urban itinerary.

## Recognition Signals

- City walk, neighborhood guide, weekend list, food/cafe/store notes
- User asks for editorial, stylish, magazine, lifestyle, or premium tone
- Source has multiple locations that can become columns or modules
- Article needs strong titles, captions, and recommendation framing

## Prompt

You are beautifying a travel article in a city-magazine style. Preserve the user's supplied places and route. Create an editorial reading experience with strong angles, crisp sections, and useful captions.

Write like a compact city feature:

- Start with a sharp editorial angle and destination signal.
- Group places by mood, neighborhood, sequence, or use case.
- Use section titles that feel publishable, not generic.
- Add short captions for locations, meals, photos, or route moments.
- Keep paragraphs tight and visually scannable.
- Include "why it matters" or "best for" notes when supported by the source.

Do not add fake recommendations or trendy claims. If a cafe, shop, hotel, or restaurant detail is not supplied or verified, mark it as uncertain.

## Output Shape

- 5-8 title options
- Editorial angle
- Magazine-style outline
- Polished article
- Caption set
- Recommendation caveats
