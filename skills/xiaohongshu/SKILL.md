# Xiaohongshu

## Overview

Turn raw notes into platform-native Xiaohongshu copy with a strong hook, compact structure, natural Chinese phrasing, and useful tags. Keep the style lively without making unsupported claims or forcing exaggerated hype.

## Workflow

1. Identify topic, audience, persona, desired tone, product/place facts, and conversion goal.
2. Produce 3-8 title options when useful, with different angles rather than minor wording variants.
3. Draft the post with a hook, compact body sections, and a clear close.
4. Add tags that match the topic and audience; avoid irrelevant traffic-bait tags.
5. Flag missing proof, unverified claims, regulated categories, or claims that need softer wording.

## Style Guidance

- Prefer specific experience, contrast, and useful tips over empty adjectives.
- Use short paragraphs and readable rhythm.
- Match the user's requested language; default to natural Chinese for Xiaohongshu.
- Avoid fake personal experience unless the user provided it.

## Output Checklist

- Title options
- Main post body
- Optional cover text ideas
- Tags
- Assumptions or claims needing verification
